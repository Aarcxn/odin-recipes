Meatballs

Party hit meatballs

People go crazy for these slow cooker party meatballs, and there is never a single one leftover at the end of a party or tailgate.

  • Meatballs: 2 pounds of meatballs.
  • Cranberry sauce: 14 ounce can of whole berry cranberry sauce.
  • Chili sauce: 12 ounce bottle tomato-based chili sauce.
  • Lemon: half a juiced lemon.
  • Hot sauce: Couple drops of hot sauce, such as Tabasco.

How to make meatballs

  1. Prep: Place meatballs, cranberry sauce, chili sauce, lemon juice, and hot sauce in a slow cooker; stir to evenly distribute sauce.
  2. Cook: Cook on High until meatballs completely thawed and hot, about 3 hours.
