Easy, Two-Ingredient Pulled Pork

An easy pulled pork recipe. Use a slow cooker to make these great sandwiches using just a pork loin and your favorite BBQ sauce.

Pulled Pork ingredients

  • Pork Loin: 2&1/2 pound boneless pork loin roast.
  • BBQ sauce: 18 ounce bottle barbeque sauce.
  • Sandwich Buns: 8 large sandwich buns, split

How to make Pulled Pork

  1. Cook the pork: Place pork in a slow cooker and add enough water to cover the bottom of the slow cooker. Cook on Low for 7 hours.
  2. Shred & stir: Transfer pork to a bowl and shred with two forks. Return pork to slow cooker and stir in barbeque sauce. Continue to cook on Low for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
  3. Serve: Spoon a generous amount of pork into each roll to serve.
